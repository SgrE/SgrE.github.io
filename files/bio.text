Dr. Fanghui Liu is currently an assistant professor at University of Warwick, UK. His research interests focus on machine learning, learning theory as well as theoretical-oriented trustworthy ML. He was a recipient of AAAI24 New Faculty Highlights Award, DAAD AInet Fellowship 2024, Rising Star in AI (KAUST 2023). He have presented two tutorials at ICASSP23 on statistical learning and and CVPR23 on deep learning theory, respectively. Prior to his current position, he worked as a postdoc researcher at EPFL (Switzerland) and KU Leuven (Belgium) from 2019 to 2023. He received his PhD from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (China) at 2019.
